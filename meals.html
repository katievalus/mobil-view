My Go To Salad

Ingredients

  1. Romaine lettuce
  2. brocoli flourettes
  3. beets
  4. sweet potatoes
  5. pumpkin seeds
  6. turkey burger

Instructions

  1. Cook sweet potatoes, brocoli, and beets. My favorite way to do this is in the air frier. Set the airfrier to 390F for 15 minuets. Start with just the sweet potatoes. After 7 minuets add the brocoli. Then, after 3 more minuets add the beets. Season with olive oil, herbs, and salt.
  2. Prepare a turkey burger however you like it.
  3. Wash letuce and chop into small strips
  4. Compine in a bowl and add pumpkin seeds to the top! I like to dress with tahini-siracha dressing.

White Bean Dip

Ingreediants

  1. 1 can of cannellini beans
  2. 1 to 2 tbsp tahini
  3. 1 to 2 tbsp salt
  4. 1/4 cup olive oil
  5. 2-3 squeezes of fresh lemon

Intructions

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender and blend! Easy as that.
  2. Add more tahini, salt, or lemoon to your liking
  3. If the consistancy is too thick, add a tbsp of water.
