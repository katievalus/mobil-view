My Faovrite Breakfast Foods

It is important to start the day off strong. I always try and get lots of protein from my breakfast so I have energy for the day.

Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup frozen pineapple
  2. 1/4 cup frozen mango
  3. 1/4 cup frozen spinach
  4. 1 scoop of vegan protein
  5. 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. combine all ingredients at once and blend!

Berry Smoothie

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  2. 1/4 cup frozen cherries
  3. 1/4 cup frozen spinach
  4. 1 scoop of vegan protein
  5. 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. combine all ingredients at once and blend!

Omlet

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup brocoli
  2. 1 tbsp diced white onion
  3. 1 cup spinach
  4. Turkey sausage
  5. 3 eggs

Instructions

  1. Saute the veggies on a frying pan with an oil of your choice untill cooked
  2. Add eggs to the frying pan and let cook all the way through
  3. Once the eggs are cooked, romove from pan and fold in half to eat
  4. Best enjoyed with salt, pepper, and hot sauce
